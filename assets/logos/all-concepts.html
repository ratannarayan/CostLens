CostLens Logo Designs

5 concepts — simplistic, impactful, modern

Concept 1 — Lens Reveal

A magnifying lens reveals the hidden cost breakdown inside. The three bars (material, conversion, margin) represent zero-based costing. Clean, professional, and instantly communicates "see the real cost."

Concept 2 — Precision Target

Precision targeting meets cost analysis. The crosshair lens suggests pinpoint accuracy — finding the exact fair price. Green center = the right price found. Works brilliantly for a data-driven platform.

Concept 3 — Monogram Mark

The letter "C" becomes a magnifying lens. The handle extends naturally from the opening. Ultra-minimal, works at any size (favicon, app icon, watermark), and feels premium. Great for the app icon.

Concept 4 — Cost Layers

Transparent cost layers (material → conversion → margin) being examined through a lens. Communicates the idea of "seeing through layers of cost" — exactly what zero-based costing does. Very analytical feel.

Concept 5 — Rupee Lens (India-First)

Hexagonal "gem" lens with the ₹ symbol front and center. Unambiguously Indian, unambiguously about cost. The hexagon suggests precision engineering (nut/bolt shape). Bold and distinctive for the Indian manufacturing market.
